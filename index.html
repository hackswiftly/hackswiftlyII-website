mission statement

our core values

optimal experience
swift

our intent for this hackathon is for the best experience for all of our participants -- ranging from beginners to veterans! we will do everything we can to support our hackers to inspire, motivate, and incite them to create the future of technology.

environmentalism
nature

for our second event, we are placing heavy emphasis on saving our planet. we hope to incite real change in attempts to combat climate change and global warming, allowing us to protect the environment that inhabits all of us.

resources
thought

because hacking can be a difficult process -- from brainstorming an idea to implementing foreign technologies, we hope to have readily available resources, including mentors, speakers, and workshop hosts, who will guide hackers every step of the way.

what sponsors should expect

communication
presence

at hackswiftly, we focus our energy to make sure sponsors are informed every step of the way. we want to make sure that sponsors are not left in the dark after graciously supporting us.

branding
attention

listed in our sponsorship prospectus, we allow sponsors to promote their products through with our hackathon being an outlet. we will make sure that all added benefits will remain true before and after the event.

trust
loyalty

once sponsors place trust in us with their resources, we make sure to live up to their expectations. we will let sponsors know where their money goes towards and how their resources are managed.
